The History About Abrams M1

ACT-1: Background

The installation of gas turbine engines (gas turbines) has become a novel feature. Initial M1 and M1 armored modified M1IP used 51 times diameter 105mm rifle gun equipped with M68A1 as main gun. M1 first entered the battlefield through Saudi Arabia in the 1991 Gulf War and outperformed its rivals, Iraq, in terms of performance, with Soviet-made T-72, T-62 and T-55.

ACT-2: Technical problems

XM1

In the second stage of 11 prototype vehicle tests, problems occurred in the air filter system, transmission device, crawler and fuel supply system of gas turbines. In the second half of 1979, three XM1 vehicles were modified, and the tests proved that most of the problems were solved. Among them, the main problems are the crawler falling off and the engine inhaling dust. The former problem is solved by redesigning and adjusting the structure of the moving and suspension parts.

M1 in Snowfield Combat

The latter problem is to install reliable seals in the filter system.
But It still has some shortcomings : Its crawler life is 1100-1500km, which can not meet the operational requirements.And XT158H track is being tested and tested.

ACT3: Weapon System

Main Weapon: RH120 44-fold caliber M256 120 mm slippery bore gun
Auxiliary weapon: A M240 7.62mm parallel machine gun is installed on the right side of the main gun.
A M240 7.62mm gun is installed at the top of the turret to fill the hatch.
The commander's command tower has a standard M2 12.7mm machine gun, which can rotate 360 degrees with pitch ranging from - 10 degrees to + 65 degrees. The machine gun can rotate electronically or manually, and the pitch operation is manual.

ACT4: Propulsion System

Engine: AGT-1500 Gas Turbine (1500 horsepower) (Daxin-Lycramine Company) Transmission: X-1100-3B fully automatic transmission (Detroit Diesel Engine Company)
