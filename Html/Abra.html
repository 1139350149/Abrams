MBT

Main Battle Tank (MBT) is a tank with the ability to attack the enemy positively and frontally. It achieves the best balance of cross-country performance, firepower and defense. Its firepower and armor protection are up to or above the level of heavy tanks in the past. At the same time, it has the characteristics of good cross-country performance of medium-sized tanks. It is the basic equipment of modern armored soldiers and the main assault weapons in ground operations.

AR Family

Assault rifle is based on the requirements of modern warfare, which successfully combines the best tactical and technical performance inherent in rifle and submachine gun. Nowadays, it refers to all kinds of automatic/semi-automatic/point-shooting rifles, which can fire intermediate power bullets or small caliber rifles with an effective range of about 300-400 meters. Its characteristics are high firing speed, stable firing, moderate recoil force, short and light gun body. It is an automatic rifle with the intense firepower of a submachine gun and the shooting power close to that of an ordinary rifle.
