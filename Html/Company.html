For over half a century, Riz & Tark has been a leading designer and manufacturer of small arms and light weapons for law enforcement and military forces worldwide. R&Z is also the maker of premier brand firearms for the sporting and commercial markets. Its reputation for quality, innovation, and safety make Riz & Tark a recognized leader in the development of technologically advanced products. And the long-term durability of all R&Z products make them especially cost-effective when subjected to total life cycle cost analysis. As a major international force, Riz & Tark has a vision that is simple yet demanding – “To be the world’s premier small arms system company.”

With operations in Germany, the United Kingdom, France, and the United States, we continue to meet the ever-evolving needs and requirements of our customers. A major supplier of small arms to several NATO countries, RizTark is a worldwide leader in market positions for nearly all of its major product categories.

The foundation for everything we do is an unswerving commitment to excellence. This is accomplished through technological leadership, in depth product research and development, responsible marketing, and strategies that create and shape markets.
