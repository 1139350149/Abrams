ACT-2: Technical problems

Leopard2 in Afghan

It was renamed Panther 2A5 Main Battle Tank. The improved Leopard 2 with 120 mm L/88 slider gun has been named Leopard 2A6 main battle tank, and gun firing experiments have been carried out. The main improvements include: new enhanced armor components in the front arc of the turret; replacing the original hydraulic fire control and stabilization system with an all-electric system; improving the recoil device of 120 mm artillery to install Rhine Metal's 120 mm L/88 slider gun in the future; the captain's top PERI R17 A2 aiming at a thermal imaging channel; the TV camera and monitor screen in the rear of the car body.
